Receive Coins

You can receive coins from someone by sending your wallet address to that person.

First, you may want to derive a new wallet.

  1. In the navigation menu, go to Accounts and in the drop-down menu, click on Accounts. Or navigate to https://wallet.xx.network/#/accounts.
  2. Select an account from the list and click on the avatar to the left of the account name to copy the wallet address.
  3. You should see a notification in the top right indicating the address was copied.
  4. Send the wallet address to the person who will send you coins.
