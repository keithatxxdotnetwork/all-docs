The [Council](Governance#Council "wikilink") is an on-chain
representative 13 member body designed to represent passive stakeholders
within the xx network.

Every seven days, an election will be held for these seats utilizing the
Phragm√©n election process standard within the Substrate Ecosystem. This
election will be based upon those who receive the plurality of coins.
Any coins can be used, including those that are bonded for staking,
locked, or vesting.

The status of the current election can be checked on the
[explorer](https://wallet.xx.network/#/council).

## Voting

1.  Go to the [xx explorer](https://wallet.xx.network/) and log in with
    your account.
2.  In the navigation menu, go to *Governance* and in the drop-down
    menu, click on *Council*. Or navigate to the
    [Council](https://wallet.xx.network/#/council) page.
    ![](@site/static/img/Main_Menu_-_Governance,_Council.png "Main_Menu_-_Governance,_Council.png")
3.  On the right side, click on the *Vote* button
    ![](@site/static/img/Explorer_Vote_button.svg).
    ![](@site/static/img/Explorer_-_Governance,_Council,_Vote_Button.png "Explorer_-_Governance,_Council,_Vote_Button.png")
4.  The *vote for current candidates* window will open up, which allows
    you to rank the candidates according to your preference.
    1.  Select the account you would like to use to cast a vote.
    2.  Select the value of your vote.
    3.  Select as many candidates as you would like.
    4.  Click the *Vote* button
        ![](@site/static/img/Explorer_Vote_button.svg) to cast your vote.

    ![](@site/static/img/Explorer_-_Vote_for_Current_Candidates.png "Explorer_-_Vote_for_Current_Candidates.png")
5.  On the next page, authorize your transaction by entering your
    password and clicking the *Sign and Submit* button
    ![](@site/static/img/Explorer_Sign_and_Submit_button.svg).
    ![](@site/static/img/Explorer_-_Authorize_Transaction_(Council_Vote).png "Explorer_-_Authorize_Transaction_(Council_Vote).png")
6.  You should see your vote appear in the interface after your
    transaction is included.

## Submitting Candidacy

1.  Go to the [xx explorer](https://wallet.xx.network/) and log in with
    your account.
2.  In the navigation menu, go to *Governance* and in the drop-down
    menu, click on *Council*. Or navigate to the
    [Council](https://wallet.xx.network/#/council) page.
    ![](@site/static/img/Main_Menu_-_Governance,_Council.png "Main_Menu_-_Governance,_Council.png")
3.  On the right side, click on the *Submit candidacy* button
    ![](@site/static/img/Explorer_Submit_candidacy_button.svg).
    ![](@site/static/img/Explorer_-_Governance,_Council,_Submit_candidacy_Button.png "Explorer_-_Governance,_Council,_Submit_candidacy_Button.png")
4.  The *submit your council candidacy* window will open up.
    1.  Select the account you would like to submit candidacy for.
    2.  This is the amount that will be bonded.
    3.  Click the *Submit* button
        ![](@site/static/img/Explorer_Submit_button.svg) to continue.

    ![](@site/static/img/Explorer_-_Submit_council_candidacy.png "Explorer_-_Submit_council_candidacy.png")
5.  On the next page, authorize your transaction by entering your
    password and clicking the *Sign and Submit* button
    ![](@site/static/img/Explorer_Sign_and_Submit_button.svg).
    ![](@site/static/img/Explorer_-_Authorize_Transaction_(Submit_Candidacy).png "Explorer_-_Authorize_Transaction_(Submit_Candidacy).png")
